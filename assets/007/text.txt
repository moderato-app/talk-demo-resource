Tony Stark, also known as Iron Man, is a fictional character in the Marvel Comics universe. He was created by writer and editor Stan Lee, along with artist Don Heck, and first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. Stark is a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor who becomes a superhero after creating a suit of armor equipped with advanced technology. He uses his suit's abilities to fight against evil and protect the world. Tony Stark is known for his charismatic personality, wit, and his role as one of the founding members of the superhero team, the Avengers. He has also appeared in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, portrayed by actor Robert Downey Jr.
